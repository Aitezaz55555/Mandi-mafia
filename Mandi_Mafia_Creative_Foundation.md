## 1. CREATIVE DIRECTION DOCUMENT

### The Narrative: "The Silk Route Meets Modern Noir"
Mandi Mafia is not a typical restaurant; it is a culinary syndicate. It represents a bold, premium culinary experience that redefines the Mandi culture in Vijayawada. The creative direction blends the secretive, exclusive, and high-stakes atmosphere of a modern "noir" speakeasy with the warm, generous, and aromatic heritage of Arabian dining. 

Every pixel, image, and interactive transition must feel premium, mysterious, yet deeply welcoming. We are moving away from the bright, cartoonish, and cheap aesthetics common in casual dining, and moving towards a luxury hospitality aesthetic (comparable to Aman Resorts, Zuma, or COYA).

### Visual Moodboard & Creative Pillars
*   **Chiaroscuro & Cinematic Contrast:** The visual style leverages heavy, rich dark backgrounds (Charcoal Black) paired with striking, illuminated focal points. Dishes should look like they are emerging from shadows, hit by a warm spotlight, emphasizing steam, glistening juices, and rich spice colors.
*   **Modernized Mashrabiya (Arabian Geometry):** Traditional patterns are simplified into ultra-thin, sleek linear structures. Instead of heavy traditional lattices, we use fine-line geometric dividers (1px thickness) in Antique Gold.
*   **Atmospheric Depths:** Laying text over subtle, dark smoky textures and deep shadows to give a sense of infinite spatial depth.
*   **The Golden Hour Glow:** Lighting in photography and UI accents should mimic the warm, golden hour of the desert—soft, amber, and incredibly premium.

### Tone of Voice
*   **Mysterious yet Generous:** "A syndicate of flavor. An alliance of hospitality."
*   **Sophisticated & Decisive:** Bold headings, clear calls to action, and copy that treats Mandi with the respect of Michelin-starred cuisine.
*   **Local Pride:** Subtle, sophisticated nods to Vijayawada’s dining enthusiasts, positioning Mandi Mafia as the ultimate destination for family feasts, high-end gatherings, and celebrations.

## 2. BRAND STYLE GUIDE

### Logo Elevation Guidance
*   **Preservation:** The core identity (Arabian architecture silhouette, lantern motifs, and skyline) must remain intact but be rendered with high precision.
*   **Visual Treatment:** The logo should be displayed in metallic Antique Gold or absolute Warm Ivory. Avoid multi-color flat fills.
*   **Safe Zone:** Maintain a clear margin around the logo equal to 1.5x the width of the central lantern motif.
*   **Background Interaction:** When overlaying photography, the logo must sit on a dark overlay of at least 40% opacity to prevent contrast loss.

## 3. DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1.  **Chiaroscuro Hierarchy (Light from Shadow):** In a luxury brand, light is precious. Use Obsidian backgrounds and Surface Charcoal cards as the canvas. Highlight key focal points (the signature platters, the booking call-to-actions) with golden halos or burgundy overlays.
2.  **Structural Symmetry & Golden Accents:** Every layout should feel architectural. Use thin lines (`1px`) of Antique Gold as structural grids and separators, mimicking the elegance of high-end structural blueprints.
3.  **Generous Spatial Luxury:** Premium dining is never cramped. Padding and margins must be expansive. Let images and text blocks breathe, ensuring the user's eye moves naturally from one focal point to another.
4.  **Tactile Textures:** Use subtle background noise gradients or noise overlays (2% opacity) on dark surfaces to simulate natural materials like dark volcanic stone or hammered brass.

## 4. COMPONENT DESIGN SYSTEM

### Buttons

*   **Primary Action (Reserve a Table / Order Platter):**
    *   *Design:* Antique Gold (`#D4AF37`) fill, Obsidian Black (`#0D0D0D`) text. 
    *   *Borders:* Solid `#D4AF37`. No border radius (sharp, architectural corners) or very subtle `2px` radius.
    *   *Hover State:* Background shifts to Gold Hover (`#E9C46A`), accompanied by a soft, warm radial gold glow (`box-shadow: 0 0 15px rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.4)`).
*   **Secondary Action (View Menu / Explore Ambience):**
    *   *Design:* Transparent fill, thin Gold outline (`1px solid rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.4)`), Warm Ivory text.
    *   *Hover State:* Background fills with Primary Burgundy (`#4A0E17`) at 80% opacity, border becomes fully solid gold, text remains Warm Ivory.
*   **Text Link CTA:**
    *   *Design:* Warm Ivory text with a small, permanent Gold dot (`•`) trailing.
    *   *Hover State:* Underline slides in from left to right in Antique Gold.

### Cards

*   **Premium Food / Platter Cards:**
    *   *Structure:* Charcoal surface card (`#1A1A1A`). The top half is a 4:3 high-definition cinematic image of the Mandi.
    *   *Details:* Name in Cormorant Garamond H4, description in Muted Silver, and price in Gold.
    *   *Visual Polish:* A subtle `1px solid rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.1)` border. On hover, the image scales up (`scale(1.05)`) inside its container, and the gold border opacity increases to `0.6`.
*   **Reservation Component (The "Syndicate Registry"):**
    *   *Structure:* Deep Burgundy card gradient (`linear-gradient(135deg, #4A0E17 0%, #1A0508 100%)`) with a thin Gold outline.
    *   *Form Elements:* Full-width input fields, integrated date-time picker, and guest count selector.
*   **Testimonial / Social Proof Cards:**
    *   *Structure:* Obsidian base with a central large Gold quote mark (`“`) in Cormorant Garamond (`60px`, light opacity).
    *   *Typography:* Cursive or italic serif text, with client detail in tracked uppercase sans-serif at the bottom.
*   **Atmosphere / Gallery Cards:**
    *   *Structure:* Full-bleed cinematic photos of the restaurant dining space.
    *   *Interaction:* On hover, a dark burgundy overlay slide-up transition reveals the section name, seating capacity, and details.

### Forms & Inputs
*   *Design:* Dark Charcoal backgrounds (`#1E1E1E`). No top/left/right borders. Only a bottom border in Muted Silver (`1px solid #333333`).
*   *Interactive State:* On focus, the bottom border morphs to Antique Gold (`#D4AF37`) and expands. The floating label shifts upwards and scales down, turning Gold.

### Badges & Tags
*   *Design:* Oval pill structure, dark background with 10% opacity Gold tint. Thin gold border (`1px solid rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.3)`). Text is ultra-small, tracked uppercase gold (e.g., `CHEF'S SPECIAL`, `EXCLUSIVE`).

### Loading, Empty, and Error States
*   *Loading Skeleton:* A slow pulsing linear gradient moving from `#1A1A1A` to `#262626`.
*   *Error / Empty Booking:* Display a minimalist broken Arabian lantern icon in Antique Gold vector art, followed by a Cormorant Garamond header: "An Interruption in the Syndicate." Accompanied by a single direct-action button to call the concierge.

## 5. ANIMATION PHILOSOPHY

Animations must feel "heavy" and premium. Fast, bouncy, or frantic motions cheapen the digital experience. All motion should be smooth, progressive, and follow a cinematic timing model.

*   **Easing Curve:** Custom Cubic Bezier: `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` (Ultra-premium Out-Expo easing. Rapid deceleration that feels luxurious and precise).
*   **Page Transitions:** Sections fade in and slide up slightly (`20px`) as they enter the viewport.
*   **Hover States:** Allow `0.3s` to `0.4s` transition durations. Buttons and borders should morph smoothly, never instantly snap.
*   **Parallax Easing:** Ambient background layers (such as faint silhouette assets or smoke textures) should scroll at 20% of the normal speed, creating cinematic depth.

## 6. UX PRINCIPLES

1.  **The Three-Step Booking Rule:** A user must be able to select date, guest size, and submit a reservation request from any point of the website within three steps.
2.  **Visual Appetizer (High Sensory Load):** Before showing prices or details, hook the visitor with beautiful, full-bleed images of Arabian platters, steam, and family gatherings.
3.  **Ambience Verification:** The digital experience must showcase the physical space (premium lighting, family seating, authentic decor in Vijayawada) to build immediate dining trust.
4.  **Local Context Prominence:** Ensure operational hours, dining rules (family sections, parking availability), and directions are accessible in a single tap on mobile devices.

## 7. ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS (WCAG AA COMPLIANCE)

*   **Contrast Ratios:**
    *   Warm Ivory (`#FDFBF7`) on Obsidian Background (`#0D0D0D`) yields a contrast ratio of `17.4:1` (Passes AAA).
    *   Antique Gold (`#D4AF37`) on Obsidian Background (`#0D0D0D`) yields `5.2:1` (Passes AA).
    *   Antique Gold (`#D4AF37`) on Deep Burgundy (`#4A0E17`) yields `4.7:1` (Passes AA).
*   **Interactive Outlines:** Standard browser outlines are disabled. Instead, focus states are custom styled with a `2px` solid Antique Gold border offset by `3px` from the element.
*   **Screen Readers:** All non-text items, food graphics, and atmosphere icons require descriptive `aria-label` tags. Empty geometric dividing lines are set with `role="presentation"`.
*   **Motion Settings:** Apply global overrides for users who have requested reduced motion:
    ```css
    @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) {
      * {
        animation-duration: 0.01ms !important;
        animation-iteration-count: 1 !important;
        transition-duration: 0.01ms !important;
        scroll-behavior: auto !important;
      }
    }
    ```

## 8. RESPONSIVE STRATEGY

*   **Breakpoints:**
    *   *Mobile Portrait:* `< 480px`
    *   *Mobile Landscape / Tablet:* `481px - 768px`
    *   *Tablet Landscape / Small Laptop:* `769px - 1024px`
    *   *Desktop Base:* `1025px - 1440px`
    *   *Cinema Displays:* `> 1440px` (Max layout container width is capped at `1360px` with margins centering it).
*   **Mobile Specific Polish:**
    *   A sticky booking bar appears at the bottom of the mobile screen when the user scrolls past the hero section.
    *   Menu cards stack into single columns or scroll horizontally as a swipeable tray (to prevent endless scrolling lists).
    *   Phone numbers and map coordinates use native mobile device URI triggers (`tel:` and `geo:`).

## 9. VISUAL HIERARCHY RULES

1.  **Display Contrast:** Every layout header block must follow the "Large Serif + Small Tracked Sans-Serif" rule. 
    *   *Example:* A small, gold label saying `ESTABLISHED 2024 • VIJAYAWADA` (Outfit, 12px, 0.15em tracking) placed directly above a large headline saying `The Royal Mandi Syndicate` (Cormorant Garamond, 52px).
2.  **Whitespace Ratios:** Vertical section margins must follow a minimum padding ratio of `12vw` (12% of viewport width) on desktop, allowing each layout block to feel like a premium art exhibit.
3.  **Focal spotlights:** No more than one primary button should be visible in the user's immediate viewport at any given time. Secondary options must be styled using the outline or text-only buttons.

## 12. NAMING CONVENTIONS

To ensure code cleanliness, team alignment, and CSS manageability, we enforce a strict **BEM (Block, Element, Modifier)** class system and dry token prefixes.

### BEM CSS Class Rules
*   **Blocks:** Low-case nouns (e.g., `.menu-card`, `.booking-form`).
*   **Elements:** Separated by double underscores (`__`) (e.g., `.menu-card__image`, `.booking-form__input`).
*   **Modifiers:** Separated by double hyphens (`--`) (e.g., `.menu-card__price--highlight`, `.btn--primary`, `.btn--disabled`).

### Asset File Naming Guidelines
*   **All lowercase with hyphens:** No spaces or camelCase (e.g., `platter-mafia-special.jpg`, `bg-texture-stone.png`).
*   **Prefix by type:** 
    *   `platter-` for main food items.
    *   `bg-` for background overlays or textures.
    *   `icon-` for vector lantern, geometric details, or utility signs.
